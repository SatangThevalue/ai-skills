## Description: <br>
ADHD-friendly assistant guiding daily planning, task breakdown, time blocking, prioritization, body doubling, dopamine regulation, and emotional support. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[modestyrichards](https://clawhub.ai/user/modestyrichards) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users use this skill for ADHD-adapted daily planning, task breakdown, time management, prioritization, body doubling, routine support, and nonjudgmental emotional reframing. It is intended as productivity and executive-function scaffolding, not diagnosis, medical treatment, therapy, or crisis care. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may handle sensitive personal routines, ADHD status, treatment context, therapy context, or other health-adjacent details through memory, reminders, schedules, or task files. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing diagnosis, medication, therapy, or sensitive routine details unless the user has reviewed retention, deletion, scheduling, and file-write controls. <br>
Risk: Users may mistake planning and emotional-support guidance for medical, psychiatric, therapy, medication, or crisis advice. <br>
Mitigation: Keep responses within productivity scaffolding and self-compassion support, and direct diagnosis, treatment, medication, self-harm, severe distress, or crisis needs to qualified professionals or appropriate crisis resources. <br>
