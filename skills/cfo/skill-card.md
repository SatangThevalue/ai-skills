## Description: <br>
Be the CFO with financial planning, cash management, fundraising, capital allocation, and strategic financial leadership. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Employees, founders, and operators use this skill for CFO-style guidance on financial planning, cash management, fundraising strategy, capital allocation, board reporting, financial operations, and related strategic finance decisions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may share confidential financial records, investor materials, or credentials while seeking analysis. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid pasting sensitive financial data unless the user intentionally wants the current agent session to analyze it. <br>
Risk: Strategic finance guidance may be incorrect, incomplete, or unsuitable for a specific company, transaction, or jurisdiction. <br>
Mitigation: Escalate fundraising terms, dilution, layoffs, debt and equity decisions, acquisition pricing, compensation, and covenant negotiations to qualified human finance, legal, or tax advisors. <br>
